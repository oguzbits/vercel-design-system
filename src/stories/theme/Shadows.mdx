# Shadows & Elevation

Geist uses shadows to provide depth and emphasize layers. Shadows are primarily used on cards, modals, and floating elements like tooltips or popovers.

## Shadow Levels

Geist defines several shadow levels to represent different elevations.

## Usage

Shadows should be used sparingly to avoid visual clutter. In the Geist aesthetic, sharp borders (`1px solid var(--accents-2)`) are often preferred over shadows for simple containment.

*   **Elevation 1**: Secondary cards, search inputs.
*   **Elevation 2**: Hover states for interactive elements.
*   **Elevation 3**: Modals, dropdowns, and tooltips.
